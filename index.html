Botcoin Website

The Botcoin website is a platform designed to provide information and services related to the Botcoin cryptocurrency project.

Main Features

  • 1. Login
    A page for registered users to access their accounts. Uses an authentication system based on email and password.

  • 2. Register
    A registration form for new users to create an account. User data will be securely stored in a database (planned to use Supabase).

  • 3. Whitepaper
    An official document that explains the vision, mission, and technology behind Botcoin. Contains technical information and project strategy.

  • 4. Log Out
    An option for users to securely log out of their accounts.

  • 5. Terms of Service
    The terms and conditions governing the use of the Botcoin platform. Users must agree to them before using the services.

  • 6. Privacy Policy
    A policy explaining how user data is collected, stored, and used.

  • 7. Airdrop
    A program that allows users to receive free Botcoin as part of promotion and early adoption. Usually requires participation, such as sharing information about Botcoin.

  • 8. Pre Sale
    The initial sale phase of Botcoin before its official launch. Users can purchase Botcoin at a lower price than the market price upon listing.

  • 9. Donation
    An option for users who want to support the project through donations. Can be in the form of cryptocurrency or other payment methods.

  • 10. Help Center
    A support center for users experiencing issues or having questions about Botcoin. Includes FAQs, guides, and support contact information.

Conclusion

The Botcoin website is not just an information hub but also a place for the community to engage in its ecosystem. With features that support security and transparency, Botcoin aims to be a trustworthy project for users and investors.
